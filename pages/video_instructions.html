Video Upload Instructions


To upload videos onto our archive you have 2 options.

1) Email us at bayardrustinarchive@gmail.com with the video and the below details:

  • Your name and email.
  • The date the video was produced.
  • The title and description of the video.

OR

2) Upload your video on YouTube and copy the link. Here are some further instructions on how to do this.

Once you have the link please click on Next and fill out the form with your video link.
