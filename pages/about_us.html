About Us


Thank you for visiting this page. We are five students of COS333, a Princeton class whose semester-long project is to develop a real-world application. We found the Bayard Rustin Center for Social Justice, and decided to embark on a journey to help them build an everlasting archive for their namesake, Bayard Rustin, an advocate for gay and civil rights.
