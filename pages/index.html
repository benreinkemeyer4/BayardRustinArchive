Welcome to the Bayard Rustin Archive! This repository is hosted by the Bayard Rustin Center for Social Justice to honor the life & legacy of our namesake, Bayard Rustin. This platform will serve as where one can not only come to learn from & be inspired by his extraordinary works & public service, but also to be able to share your own materials or experiences therein. Here you'll find scholarly resources, personal stories, as well as the opportunity to contribute any material about the life of Bayard Rustin that you may have to share! In conjunction with this database, please know that you can find further highlights of Bayard Rustin materials, media, & ephemera on the BRCSJ community website.

Please make sure that this contribution to this collaborative archive of the life & legacy of Bayard Rustin is from your personal collection or available in the public domain.
